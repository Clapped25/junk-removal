Item Guide

What We Will & Won't Take

We keep it simple. If it's non-hazardous and liftable, we'll take it. Not sure? Just call and we'll let you know.

We'll Take It YES

  • ✓Sofas, chairs, bed frames, dressers
  • ✓Mattresses and box springs
  • ✓Washers, dryers, refrigerators
  • ✓TVs, computers, monitors
  • ✓Lawn mowers, outdoor furniture
  • ✓Wood, drywall, concrete (small amounts)
  • ✓Boxes, bags, general clutter
  • ✓Hot tubs and exercise equipment

We Can't Take NO

  • ✗Hazardous chemicals or paint
  • ✗Gasoline, motor oil, propane tanks
  • ✗Asbestos or lead-containing materials
  • ✗Medical / biohazard waste
  • ✗Car batteries (call for options)
  • ✗Anything illegal to dispose of
